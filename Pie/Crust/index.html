Better Manage Your Online Status

StatusPeople aim to help you better manage your online status. We are developing tools to allow you to easily interact with your users, subscribers and customers online.

We will achieve this by developing web tools for publishing content, managing data and interacting with social media sites such as Facebook and Twitter.

To view some of the Social Media tools we are developing please view our tools page. Access to our social media tools is free all you need to do is sign up for a StatusPeople account.

For more information about our tools please contact us at info@statuspeople.com
